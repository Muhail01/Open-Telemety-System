# Code of Conduct

Open Telemetry System contributors are expected to keep project spaces professional, inclusive, and focused on constructive technical collaboration.

We do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disclosure of private information, or sustained disruptive behavior.

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, restrict participation, or take other reasonable moderation actions when behavior harms contributors or the project.

When discussing ranking, recommendation, experimentation, or marketplace behavior, contributors should distinguish technical claims from product policy and should not use the project to facilitate unlawful discrimination or privacy-invasive targeting.

Concerns should be reported privately to the project maintainers rather than escalated through public argument.
